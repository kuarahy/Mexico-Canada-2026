## 📥 How to Download

No installation required. No technical knowledge needed. Just follow these steps:

**Step 1 —** Click the green **`< > Code`** button near the top of this page

**Step 2 —** Click **`Download ZIP`**

**Step 3 —** Find the downloaded ZIP file (usually in your **Downloads** folder), right-click it, and choose **Extract All** (Windows) or double-click it (Mac)

**Step 4 —** Open the extracted folder and double-click **`worldcup2026.html`**

That's it! It opens in your web browser like a regular website. ✅

> 💡 **Tip:** You only need to do this once. Keep the folder somewhere handy and just double-click the file whenever you want to check scores.

> 🌐 **Internet connection** is needed to display the team flags. Everything else works offline.

## ⚽ What Can I Do With It?

### Enter Match Scores

1. Click on any match
2. A small window pops up — type in the final score
3. For knockout matches, there are extra fields for penalty shootouts
4. Click **Save**
5. The standings table updates automatically!

### Three Views to Explore

| Tab | What's there |
|---|---|
| ⚽ **Group Stage** | All 12 groups, every match, and the live standings table |
| 🏆 **Knockout** | The full bracket — from Round of 32 all the way to the Final |
| 🗓️ **Schedule** | Every single match listed by date, with kickoff times |

### Filter the Schedule

At the top of the Schedule tab you'll find buttons to show:
- All matches
- Group Stage matches only
- Knockout matches only
- Upcoming matches (not played yet)
- Completed matches

### Your Scores Are Saved Automatically 💾

Every score you enter is saved directly in your browser. You can close the tab, turn off your computer, and come back later — your data will still be there.
